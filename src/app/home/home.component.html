Spry is a revolutionary, cloud-based operating system that enables the movement health professional (physiatrist, physical therapist, athletic trainer, recovery specialist) to seamlessly capture and assess patient care data in real time. Cutting edge technologies such as machine learning and AI help Spry to provide patient specific protocols based on evidence-based practice and standards of care India is facing a MSK crisis with more than 25% of the population experiencing chronic pain and an explosion in knee surgeries (15 times the western world). We use Computer Vision and Deep Learning to automate assessments, adherence to programs and measure recovery.
